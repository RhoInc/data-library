The Analysis Data Model, ADaM for short, is a clinical trial data and metadata standard established by CDISC that supports:
  • the efficient generation, replication, and review of clinical trial statistical analyses
  • traceability among analysis results, analysis data, and data reprecented in the Study Data Tabulation Model (SDTM)
The datasets below adhere to ADaM and comprise simulated data used to develop Rho's clinical safety and data cleaning graphics.
